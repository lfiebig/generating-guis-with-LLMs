Click the "Toggle Device Toolbar" button to simulate mobile devices or press `Ctrl+Shift+M` (Cmd+Shift+M on Mac). 5. Select the device with a screen size of 375 x 667 pixels from the dropdown menu at the top of the screen, or manually set the dimensions.
