Delicious Lasagna

by Chef Gordon

Rating: ★★★★☆

Cook Time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese

Preparation Steps:

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet.
  2. Layer ingredients in a baking dish.
  3. Bake for 45 minutes at 375°F.

Utensils and Equipment:

  • Skillet
  • Baking dish
  • Measuring cups
